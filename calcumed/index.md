# Privacy Policy for Calcumed

**Last updated: March 13, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Calcumed** ("the App") handles your information. 

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**Calcumed** is designed to prioritize user privacy. 
* **Personal Data:** The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location data.
* **Usage Data:** The App does **not** use third-party analytics (like Firebase or Google Analytics). All calculations are performed locally on your device.
* **Technical Setup:** As a web-based application encapsulated via Capacitor, the App runs as a local static site. No data is sent to external servers during its operation.

## 2. Permissions
The App requires no special permissions (camera, microphone, contacts, or storage) to function. If any standard web permissions are requested by the underlying system, they are not used to collect user data.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App does **not** contain advertisements, social media plugins, or other third-party services that could track your activity.

## 4. Medical Disclaimer
**Calcumed** is a tool intended for educational and clinical support purposes for healthcare professionals. 
* The results provided by the calculators are based on clinical formulas but should **not** replace professional medical judgment. 
* We do not store any patient data entered into the calculators. Once the App is closed or the page is refreshed, all entered values are cleared.

## 5. Children's Privacy
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
